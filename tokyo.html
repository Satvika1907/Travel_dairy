About Tokyo

Tokyo is a dazzling metropolis where ultra-modern towers meet quiet Shinto shrines, lively neighbourhoods mix with serene parks, and world-class cuisine is around every corner.

From neon-lit Shinjuku to historic Asakusa, Tokyo blends cutting-edge culture with centuries of tradition.

Tokyo is the capital and largest city of Japan, located on the island of Honshu, and serves as the country's center for government, business, culture, and industry. Known for blending modern and traditional aspects, it features historic temples like SensÅ-ji alongside neon-lit skyscrapers and is the heart of the world's most populous metropolitan area. A hub for dining, shopping, and entertainment, Tokyo offers attractions from the Imperial Palace and numerous museums to fashion districts like Shibuya and the futuristic Tokyo Skytree.
